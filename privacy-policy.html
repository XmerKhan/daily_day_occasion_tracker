Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Vidify is built with privacy at its core. We do not collect, sell or share personal data.

Location Data

We use IP geolocation services (ipwho.is, ip-api.com, ipapi.co) solely to detect your country for localized content. Your IP address is not stored on our servers.

Local Storage

Only your gender preference and optional country choice are saved locally in your browser's localStorage. You can clear them anytime by clearing your browser data. We do not have access to this data.

No Accounts

Vidify has no sign-up or login. We do not collect names, email addresses or any personal identifiers — unless you voluntarily contact us via email.

Third-Party APIs

We query the following external services to provide our features:

  • Calendarific — Country-specific public holidays
  • Aladhan — Accurate Islamic (Hijri) dates
  • ipwho.is / ip-api.com / ipapi.co — IP-based country detection

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Advertising

Vidify may display ads via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

We do not set our own tracking cookies. Any cookies present are from Google AdSense for ad personalization purposes.

Children's Privacy

Vidify is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at contact@vidify.site.
