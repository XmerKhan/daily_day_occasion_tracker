About Us

Vidify is a free daily companion that tells you what special day it is today — anywhere in the world. From globally recognized celebrations like Eid, Christmas and New Year, to national independence days, awareness days and over 300 cultural moments curated from around the world.

Our Mission

We believe every day deserves to be noticed. Vidify surfaces today's most relevant occasion for your country and gives you a warm, personalized wish message to share with the people who matter most — in a single tap.

How We Work

We combine three trusted data sources:

  • Hand-curated database — 300+ worldwide occasions, holidays and cultural moments.
  • Calendarific API — Accurate country-specific public holidays for every nation.
  • Aladhan API — Authentic Hijri (Islamic) dates calculated astronomically.

Our Values

  • Privacy first — No accounts, no tracking, no personal data stored on our servers.
  • Free forever — Vidify is and always will be free to use.
  • Human warmth — Every wish message is hand-written, not AI-generated filler.
  • Speed — No apps to install. Works instantly in any browser.
