FAQ

How does Vidify know what special day it is today?

We combine three sources — a curated worldwide occasions database, the Calendarific API for country-specific holidays, and the Aladhan API for accurate Islamic dates.

Are wish messages different for every person?

Yes. Messages are personalized by occasion and selected gender, with five variations each so you can cycle through them.

Can I share these on WhatsApp?

Absolutely. Tap Copy and paste anywhere, or use Download to save the wish as an image to share on stories.

How are Islamic dates detected accurately?

We use the Aladhan API which converts Gregorian dates to Hijri based on astronomical calculation, the same standard used worldwide.

Does Vidify store my location or personal data?

No. We only read your country code in the browser to localize content. Nothing is stored on a server.

Privacy Policy

Vidify is built with privacy at its core. We do not collect, sell or share personal data.

  • Location: We use ipapi.co solely to detect your country for localized content. Your IP is not stored on our servers.
  • localStorage: Only your gender preference is saved locally in your browser for personalized wishes. You can clear it anytime.
  • No accounts: Vidify has no sign-up. We do not collect names, emails or any personal identifiers (unless you voluntarily contact us).
  • Third-party APIs: We query Calendarific (holidays), Aladhan (Islamic dates) and ipapi.co (country detection). Each has its own privacy policy.
  • Advertising: Vidify may display ads via Google AdSense, which may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can manage ad preferences via Google's settings.
  • Cookies: We do not set our own tracking cookies.
